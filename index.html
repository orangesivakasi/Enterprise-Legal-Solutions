About Us

Enterprise Legal Solutions was formed to provide clients with prompt, responsive and responsible legal advice and solutions. The Firm is founded by Mr. Rahul Kandampully and Ms. Raaga Ramalakshmi, both of whom have a decade of practice particularly in the field of Litigation, Corporate and Intellectual Property Laws. The Firm has now successfully branched on to Maritime, Arbitration, Environment, Tax and Real Estate Solutions with local partners. The firm currently practices in the High Courts and District Courts of the State of Tamil Nadu & Kerala, India.
